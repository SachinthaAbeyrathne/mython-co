A BIT ABOUT US

Mythicon Co. is a growing company which creates technology- based toys with the aim of teaching kids to code while bringing fun to their playtime. By taking the form of mythical creatures such as Unicorns, Dragons etc., the Mythicon robot® toy allows kids to control their robot’s movements, lights & sounds by entering different codes through the “Mythicode” app. (Can download the app on compatible smart phones & tablets for free).Designed using colorful LED lights, sensors & sounds, these toys offer a variety of fun coding challenges, improving their programming skills.

The Mythicon Pegasus™.

This time, the toy robot takes form of the magical creature Pegasus and will also be introduced with different color sets & sizes. Mythicon Pegasus can also be controlled with the Mythicode app allowing kids to learn and play through fun coding challenges & special missions..
